▸ WHY TERMINAL GAMES?

Terminal games are accessible - they work over SSH, on servers, on old hardware, and with screen readers. No GPU required.

They're portable - pure Python, no compilation, works on Linux, macOS, and WSL. No dependencies beyond the standard library.

They're hackable - just text files. Easy to read, modify, fork. Version control friendly. Educational.

And they're beautiful - there's elegance in constraints. Retro computing nostalgia. ANSI art. Minimalist, functional design.

▸ WHY HUMAN/AI COLLABORATION?

Because it works. Donovan provides vision, design sense, and understanding of what makes games fun. Claude provides implementation speed, architectural knowledge, and documentation thoroughness.

Together, we shipped colony.sh v0.1 alpha in 6 hours - from concept to playable game with complete documentation. That's not just fast execution; it's true collaboration.

We're transparent about this process because hiding it would be dishonest. People are divided about AI-assisted development, often due to fear. We believe in showing that when done thoughtfully, human + AI collaboration can produce real craft.
