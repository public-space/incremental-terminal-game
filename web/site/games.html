â–¸ FEATURES

  • Idle Progression - Resources generate while you work, sleep, or explore other tasks
  • 5 Structures - Mining Rigs, Solar Arrays, Hydroponics, Fabricators, Habitats
  • 4 Research Upgrades - Unlock efficiency improvements and new capabilities
  • Dark Sci-Fi Lore - Fragmented logs, mysterious signals, ominous discoveries
  • Terminal Native - Beautiful ANSI colors, box-drawing characters, clean animations
  • Save/Load System - JSON-based persistence, resume anytime
  • Data-Oriented Design - Built on Lattice Engine, easy to mod and extend
  • No Dependencies - Pure Python, works on Linux, macOS, WSL
